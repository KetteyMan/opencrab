# OpenCrab UI Notes

## Desired Feel
- Calm but capable
- Dense enough for serious work, breathable enough for long sessions
- Editorial rather than template-driven
- Productized, not a collection of demo cards

## Common Failure Modes
- Too many rounded containers competing at once
- Header area saying the same thing three times
- Long explanatory text on the primary surface
- Badges and pills carrying more emphasis than the actual task content
- Decorative gradients or shadows hiding weak hierarchy

## Useful Moves
- Compress redundant meta into compact pills
- Let one title row do the job of three sections
- Use one strong accent and many quiet surfaces
- Give critical controls a sharper contrast step than informational chips
- Hide verbose details behind drawers, dialogs, or expandable rows
